About AMK Care

Care should feel personal, respectful and reassuring. AMK Care supports people who wish to remain comfortable and independent at home.

Our approach

AMK Care provides compassionate, non-clinical live-in care and support at home. The focus is on dignity, comfort, independence, companionship and clear communication with families.

Care built around the person

Every person has their own routine, preferences, needs and home life. AMK Care aims to shape support around the individual rather than forcing people into a fixed routine.
