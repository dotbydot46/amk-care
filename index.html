FAQs

Questions families often ask

Simple answers to help you understand live-in care quickly.

What is live-in care?

Live-in care means a carer lives in the customer’s home and provides support according to the agreed Care Plan.

How much does AMK Care cost?

Live-in care starts from £1,200 per week. The final cost depends on care needs, the care plan and any additional support required.

Is live-in care suitable for couples?

Live-in care can support couples who wish to remain together at home, depending on the care needs and agreed plan.

Does the carer need their own room?

Yes. For live-in care, the carer needs a suitable private bedroom and access to bathroom and kitchen facilities.

How do I start?

Complete the free consultation form, call AMK Care, send an email, or use WhatsApp. We will help you understand the next step.
