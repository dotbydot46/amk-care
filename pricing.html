Personalised care quotation

Every person’s care needs are different. Following a free assessment, AMK Care Service will provide a personalised quotation based on the care requirements, location, type of care and level of support needed.

Why we do not show one fixed price

Care can vary depending on the person’s routine, support needs, whether care is live-in or home care, and any additional arrangements required. A personalised quotation helps families understand the right level of support before care begins.

What the assessment may consider

  • Type of care required
  • Level of daily or overnight support
  • Personal care, mobility or medication prompting needs
  • Location and availability
  • Individual preferences, routines and family expectations
