# AMK Care CRM Testing Checklist

After connecting the Google Apps Script URL:

## Client enquiry test
- Open contact page.
- Fill in the care enquiry form with test details.
- Submit by email button.
- Check Client Enquiries tab in Google Sheet.
- Check AMK email inbox for notification.
- Check thank-you page opens.

## Carer application test
- Open Join AMK Care page.
- Fill in the carer application form with test details.
- Submit form.
- Check Carer Applications tab in Google Sheet.
- Check AMK email inbox for notification.

## WhatsApp test
- Fill in either form.
- Click WhatsApp option.
- Confirm WhatsApp opens with a full message.
- Confirm the lead also appears in Google Sheet when endpoint is connected.

## Final checks
- Change test lead status in Sheet.
- Add follow-up date.
- Add notes.
- Delete test submissions before launch if needed.
