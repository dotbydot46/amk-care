Our Services

Care and support shaped around daily life.

AMK Care provides compassionate, non-clinical support designed around the person, the home and the family.

Live-in Care

Support at home with daily routines, reassurance, companionship and ongoing help based on the agreed care plan.

Companionship

Conversation, emotional reassurance, meaningful activities, outings and reducing loneliness at home and in the community.

Personal Care Support

Respectful support with washing, dressing, grooming, mobility, toileting and daily comfort where agreed.

Meals & Daily Living

Meal preparation, drinks, light household tasks, appointments, reminders and everyday support around the home.

Respite Care

Short-term support when family carers need rest, travel, recovery time or temporary additional help.

Couples Care

Support for couples who wish to remain together at home, with care shaped around both people’s needs.
