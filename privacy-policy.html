Privacy Policy

How AMK Care Service handles personal information.

This page explains how website enquiry and application data may be collected and used.

Privacy Policy

AMK Care Service respects your privacy and will use personal information only for appropriate care enquiry, recruitment and communication purposes.

Information we may collect

  • Contact details submitted through the website
  • Care enquiry information such as location, type of care and message
  • Carer application information such as experience, availability and right-to-work confirmation
  • Website analytics data where consent is given

How information is used

Information is used to respond to enquiries, manage carer applications, keep records, improve services and meet legal or safeguarding obligations where applicable.

CRM and email notifications

Website form submissions may be saved into AMK Care Service’s private Google Sheet/CRM and may generate email notifications to authorised AMK Care Service staff.

Your rights

You can contact AMK Care Service to ask about your data, request correction or request deletion where legally appropriate.

Privacy contact: help@amkcare.co.uk

This policy should be reviewed by AMK Care Service before final launch to ensure it matches real internal data handling practices.
