Become an AMK Carer

AMK Care Service welcomes caring, reliable and compassionate people who want to support individuals and families with dignity, patience and respect.

Applicants should ideally have

  • Right to work in the UK
  • A caring and compassionate nature
  • Good communication skills
  • Enhanced DBS, or willingness to obtain one
  • Relevant care experience preferred
  • Willingness to complete mandatory training
  • Reliable and professional attitude

This first-stage form is only for initial interest. Please do not send passports, National Insurance numbers, certificates or sensitive documents through the website. AMK Care Service can request documents later through a secure process.
