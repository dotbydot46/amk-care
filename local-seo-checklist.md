# AMK Care Local SEO Checklist

Before final launch, confirm and add:

1. Official company name, address, company number and service areas.
2. Real towns/counties covered by AMK Care.
3. Google Business Profile with the same name, phone, website and service areas.
4. Real photos from AMK Care, with consent.
5. Real reviews from families, with permission.
6. Location pages only for places AMK truly serves, for example:
   - Live-in Care in [Town]
   - Live-in Care in [County]
   - Couples Live-in Care in [Town]
7. Connect Google Search Console after launch.
8. Add Google Analytics or another privacy-friendly analytics tool.
9. Make sure all pages use the same contact number: 07852 888 932.
10. Do not publish CQC, DBS, insurance or regulated-status claims until confirmed.
